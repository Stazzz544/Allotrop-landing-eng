For many years, the planet RG-27 served as the port point of the Allotrop Galaxy and was a peripheral region for the production of platinum, gold and red diamonds. When in 3052 lanthanum (La) was discovered in its bowels...
It acquired the status of an industrial settlement of the 6A type and became the main producer of rare earth metals. However, 200 years ago, during the expansion of neighboring Galaxies, RG-27 was occupied by military squadrons...
And the inhabitants decided to provoke a large chemical explosion, which led to a change in the atmosphere of the planet, making it uninhabitable, but helped to preserve the classified technologies of the settlers...
...

REALITY ON YOUR TERMS

You have to go alone to an abandoned industrial planet, try on your role as an engineer and decipher the classified projects of its settlers. Survive in conditions of limited resources, emergencies and frightening natural phenomena. To restore the main infrastructure facilities, establish the work of combine cities and production plants, bring out the best varieties of food crops, automate mining and return RG-27 status of the central industrial planet of the Allotrop galaxy.

IMMERSIVE LEARNING:

Story puzzles, complemented by original development of game designers, fascinating research and game tasks, which include materials in physics, mathematics, biology and programming, provide an opportunity to increase the level of understanding of the natural sciences by 17%.

SCIENTIFIC RESEARCH:

The game is based on the modern educational approach Game-Based Learning, recognized by UNESCO and teachers around the world, which allows you to create complex engineering devices and conduct scientific experiments in game conditions close to laboratory ones.

REAL CONDITIONS:

In the process of passing the levels, the player gets access to new knowledge and effective ways to solve his tasks, contacts innovative developments and materials of the future, and also has the opportunity to gain practical skills that will be useful to him in the real world.

PRACTICAL SKILLS:

Developed with an emphasis on science and realism, the game helps establish a connection between cause and effect in understanding complex physical phenomena, trains logic, attention and memory, helps the player develop critical thinking, emotional intelligence and the habit of working on the result.
